About Casa Lumière

Founded in 2015, Casa Lumière began with a simple vision: to bring the calming essence of forest green into modern living spaces. Our name, meaning "House of Light," reflects our commitment to creating homes that are both beautiful and uplifting.

We specialize in sustainable home décor that harmonizes with nature. Our forest green palette is carefully curated to evoke feelings of peace, balance, and connection to the natural world.

Every piece in our collection is sourced from eco-friendly materials and crafted by artisans who share our passion for quality and sustainability. From furniture to accessories, each item is designed to transform your house into a serene sanctuary.

  1. Contact our customer service team at returns@casalumiere.com or call (555) 123-4567 to obtain a Return Authorization Number (RAN).

Refund processing 3-5 business days
Credit appearing on your statement 3-10 business days (depending on financial institution)

If you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact us within 7 days of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or refund at no additional cost to you. You may be asked to provide photographs of the damage or defect.

7. Shipping Costs

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, except in cases of damaged, defective, or incorrect items shipped by us. For these exceptions, we will provide a prepaid return shipping label.

8. Store Credit

If you prefer, we can issue store credit instead of a refund to your original payment method. Store credit never expires and can be used for future purchases on our website.

International customers are responsible for all customs duties, taxes, and shipping charges associated with returns. We recommend using a trackable shipping service and purchasing shipping insurance.

10. Sale Items

Sale items can be returned within 14 days of delivery for store credit only.

11. Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund within the expected timeframe, please:

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to the website owners.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes, including:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly

  • Functionality Cookies: Remember choices you make to improve your experience
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements
